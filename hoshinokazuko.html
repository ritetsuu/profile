Special Summoner Profile

Hoshino Kazuko is the current leader of the Huon Board, which governs the world of Huon. He grants special passes and complete immunity to the rest of the Special Summoners. He is the Special Summoner of Creation, and the fourth strongest summoner of all time.

Background

Kazuko, Special Summoner of Creation, is friendly and authorative, albeit stubborn. He has a net worth of 25 million, born into an upper-class family and working at the Huon Board. Kazuko studied at New Hampton, Remus University.

Childhood

Kazuko's grandparents were the leaders of the Huon Board, and left the position to Kazuko.
