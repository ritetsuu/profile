Hakuri Ritetsu

Special Summoner Profile

Hakuri Ritetsu is one of the three main characters, starring alongside Kasumi Kurogane and Shiro Seiji. He is the Special Summoner of Order, and the strongest summoner of all time.

Background

Ritetsu, Special Summoner of Order, is decisive and calm-headed, albeit condescending and merciless. He has a net worth of 35 million, born into an upper-class family. Ritetsu studied at Godfall, Bachina University. He owns the company Hakuri Manufacturing.

Childhood

His parents divorced when he was 5, and he lived with his father and brother ever since. Friends and neighbours describe him as harsh and strict, as well as being unfriendly and introverted. Ritetsu aced his studies, and attained a scholarship at Bachina University before inheriting the company.
