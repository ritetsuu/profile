Rogu Kurai

Special Summoner Profile

Rogu Kurai is the oldest in all of the eight summoners, and works as the Huon Freepact School He is the Special Summoner of Destruction, and is the first summoner to be able to use both Destruction and a Chaos style.

Background

Rogu, Special Summoner of Destruction, is wise and friendly, albeit hot-headed and aggressive. He has a net worth of 35 million, born into an upper-class family. Ritetsu studied at Godfall, Bachina University. He owns the company Hakuri Manufacturing.

Childhood

Rogu started as a judo and martial arts student from young.
