Seiji Shiro

Special Summoner Profile

Seiji Shiro is one of the three main characters, starring alongside Kasumi Kurogane and Hakuri Ritetsu. He is the Special Summoner of Order, and the third strongest summoner of all time.

Background

Seiji, Special Summoner of Order, is curious and physically strong, albeit having fragile ego and confidence and indecisiveness.

Childhood

His parents died in a car crash when he was young, and they were unidentifiable. Iris Coalman took pity on young Seiji and adopted him. Seiji is currently studying at Takida High School.
