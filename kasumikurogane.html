Kasumi Kurogane

Special Summoner Profile

Kasumi Kurogane is one of the three main characters, starring alongside Hakuri Ritetsu and Shiro Seiji. She is the Special Summoner of Chaos, and the second strongest summoner of all time.

Background

Kasumi, Special Summoner of Chaos, is relaxed and warm, albeit indifferent and unserious. She has a net worth of 15 million, born into an upper-class family. Kasumi studied at Godfall, Bachina University. She works as a full time summoner.

Childhood

Kasumi is described by friends as happy and outgoing, but an encounter with Shane Takagi left her scarred mentally and emotionally.
